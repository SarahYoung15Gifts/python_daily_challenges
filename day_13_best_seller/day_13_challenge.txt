The Scenario:
You are helping a small shop review a day's sales. Each sale record includes an item name, quantity sold, and price per item. Your job is to write a program that calculates totals and finds the best-selling item.

Your Task:

Use this data structure:
sales = [
	{"item": "Notebook", "quantity": 4, "price": 2.50},
	{"item": "Pen", "quantity": 10, "price": 1.20},
	{"item": "Backpack", "quantity": 2, "price": 18.00},
	{"item": "Notebook", "quantity": 3, "price": 2.50}
]

Create a function called calculate_sale_total(sale) that returns the total value of one sale.

Loop through the sales list and print each item in this format:
Notebook: 4 x $2.50 = $10.00

Keep track of the overall revenue and print it at the end in this format:
Total Revenue: $65.50

Also determine which item sold the highest total quantity across all sales records.

Print the result in this format:
Best-Selling Item: Pen

Bonus:

If two items tie for the highest quantity sold, print both item names.
